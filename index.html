Gomenasai, my name is Vergil-Dono.
I'm a 45 year old half-demon samurai (swordsman for you gaijins). I wrote poetry on my book, and spend my days perfecting my art and playing superior Japanese games. (Samurai Shodown, Final Fantasy, Sekiro) I train with my Yamato every day, this superior weapon can cut clean through steel because it is folded over a thousand times, and is vastly superior to any other devil arm. I acquired my sword when I was 8, and I have been getting better every day. I speak Japanese fluently, both Kanji and the Osaka dialect, and I write fluently as well. I know everything about Japanese history and their bushido code, which I follow 100% When I acquire my father's sword, I am moving to the underworld to fight more powerful demons and get more power. I hope I can defeat my brother or the demon king Mundus I own several long trenchcoats, which I wear around town. I want to get used to wearing them before I move to the demon world, so I can fit in easier. I attack my brother and son and quote poetry as often as I can, but rarely does anyone manage to respond. Wish me luck in the demon world!
